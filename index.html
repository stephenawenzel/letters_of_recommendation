When should I have my letter completed?

While each college has their own deadline, it is best to have your letter completed by the last week of May.

Where do I send/submit my letter?

I will be using Interfolio to collect letters of recommendation. The platform allows me to send an email to you when you are ready to submit your letter. From there, the program will guide you through the process! If you would like to send your letter through the mail, please contact me. Important: I will not read your letters.
